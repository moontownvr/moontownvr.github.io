WHAT IS MOONTOWN?

Moontown is an Ethereum blockchain-based VR experience platform or a metaverse project whose idea is to build virtual cities on the moon. The concept merges elements of a play to earn game, a new type of social network, and a blockchain marketplace of Visual Arts, Architecture, 3D models & graphics, Design, Music, etc. 
The moontown will be developed by its participants. 

Libermol City in a moon crater

Libermol City

Libermol is a first virtual city-state on the moon founded and designed in 3d by Architexyz. The virtual prototype of this city is represented by a unique architectural environment where modern urban space, buildings and structures are in harmony with natural landscapes, trees, greens and water. The city has buildings of art galleries, trade and exhibition centers, virtual offices, a science center, etc.

HOW IT WORKS?

At the first stage of the project, the moontown will sell a virtual property in the form of land parcels, architecture such as buildings and interior spaces, visual and sound art as non-fungible tokens. This will allow us to accumulate funds for further development. The next stage involves the creation of an interactive environment, with the ability to walk in the 3D town space by using VR headset. The final purpose is the creation of a full-fledged metaverse including online multiplayer, virtual chats and showrooms, interactive architecture, space for meditation, and  a possibility for users to create their own activities.

TECHNICAL BASE

ETHEREUM

Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain with smart contract functionality. Ethereum allows anyone to deploy permanent and immutable decentralized applications onto it, with which users can interact

UNREAL ENGINE

Powerful and production-proven, Unreal Engine is designed for demanding applications such as AAA games, filmmaking, and photoreal visualization, making it ideally suited to these challenging requirements.

NFT

An NFT is a non-fungible token; an intangible digital asset in the form of images, video, or another items.
These virtual assets come with certificates that show ownership and are influencing the growth of the metaverse.

VR

Virtual Reality (VR) is a computer-generated environment with scenes and objects that appear to be real, making the user feel they are immersed in their surroundings.
