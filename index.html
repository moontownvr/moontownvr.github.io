WHAT IS MOONTOWN?

Moontown is a metaverse project whose idea is creating virtual cities on the moon. The concept merges elements of a "play to earn" game, a new type of social network, and a blockchain marketplace of Visual Arts, Architecture, 3D models & CG graphics, Design, Music, etc. Moontown allows users to create businesses, represent their social activities, ideas, researches and art,  buy and sell digital assets, provide and use services, own virtual land, real estate and spaces


Libermol City in the moon crater

LIBERMOL

Libermol is a first virtual city-state on the moon founded and designed in 3D by Architexyz. The virtual prototype of this city is represented by a unique architectural environment where modern urban space, buildings and structures are in harmony with natural landscapes, trees, greens and water. The city has buildings of art galleries, trade and exhibition centers, virtual offices, a science center, etc

MISSION

The project mission is to unite people and companies on humanistic values in modern world challenges such as freedom of human rights, financial independence and sustainable evolution. Moontown is based on the idea of co-building, very similar to the Tower of Babel construction in the far past. Moontown synthesizes together all creative fields  such as IT, arts & sciences, design, architecture, philosophy, ecology, etc. Moontown is a future home for many and many projects, provide them virtual environment developed by world-class professional architects and visual graphic designers

BLUEPRINT

The first stage of the Moontown project involves the accumulation of funds through the sale of virtual land plots, architecture 3D models , interior spaces and digital art. The second stage involves the creation servers located virtual environment, with interactive walkthrough option in the city spaces by using VR headset. On the third stage the Moontown will be a full-fledged metaverse with VR immersive experience including online multiplayer, virtual chats and showrooms, and options for users to create their own activities

3D GENERATIVE AI 

To accelerate the development of 3D worlds and the metaverse, NVIDIA has launched numerous AI research projects to help creators across industries unlock new possibilities with generative AI. Announced at CES, GET3D is a new generative AI model that generates 3D shapes with topology, rich geometric details, and textures from data like 2D images, text, and numbers

UNREAL ENGINE

Powerful and production-proven, Unreal Engine is designed for demanding applications such as AAA games, filmmaking, and photoreal visualization, making it ideally suited to these challenging requirements

UNITY

Unity is a cross-platform game engine which support a variety of desktop, mobile, console and virtual reality platforms. It is particularly popular for iOS and Android mobile game development, is considered easy to use for beginner developers, and is popular for indie game development

ETHEREUM

Ethereum is a decentralized blockchain with smart contract functionality. Ethereum allows anyone to deploy permanent and immutable decentralized applications onto it, with which users can interact

NFT

An NFT is a non-fungible token; an intangible digital asset in the form of images, video, or another items.
These virtual assets come with certificates that show ownership and are influencing the growth of the metaverse.
